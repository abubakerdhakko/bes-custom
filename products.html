Explorer

Our Explorer product is designed to give you the power to manage everything from the contract and token transactions to token transfers and user accounts. In short, our Explorer brings you everything you need to make your cryptocurrency project an absolute success. There are three main areas of our Explorer and they include a Dashboard featuring all information related to Contract Transactions and Token Transactions, a Transaction Page displaying Token Transfers, Contract Transactions, and Transactions Page Navigator, and an Accounts Page featuring Accounts Page Navigator. All these features allow you to manage your blockchain-based cryptocurrency project and make it a booming success.

Explorer Price: 3900.00$

Core Benefits

Core Benefits of BES Explorer allows you to monitor smart contracts with TxN Hash, Block Number, and Timestamp, and also search items using account address, transaction hash, and block number all on a single screen. The dashboard of BES Explorer also shows the marker cap for the configured coins and a chart that displays the coin trend in the past months. The Explorer further displays the information related to the token transfers being made including the TxHash, Value, Block, Age, From and To, and Gas Price of each transaction. There is also an Accounts Page that displays the records involving the account addresses that have been involved in the token transactions for the configured token.
