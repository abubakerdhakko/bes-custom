7 GOLDEN RULES MANAGERS CAN USE TO MOTIVATE THEIR EMPLOYEES

We all know to make a business work & then ultimately successful demands multiple efforts, not one man can make it possible. Known to this fact every manager tend to hire the best employees. But in this process they forget the fact that employees belong to the same kingdom as of theirs and they’re very much human beings and human beings always need time to time motivation to work efficiently. Managers more often misinterpret it with the stereotypical phrase that money is the only motivation which let one perform with proficiency. This leads to major managerial failures. To cater that we’ve enlisted seven golden rules which could be implied in order to motivate your employees.

  1. Hear

The art of communication is the jack of all traits when it comes to dealing with your workers. As every human being demands respect and want their opinion to be heard so they want to be the part of your important decisions. This refers to the psychology of human mind that if it is given the desired importance it often produces the desired results. So if the managers take out time to hear their problems, discuss ideas, socialize and hear concerns related to their jobs will integrate a feeling of importance in them that they’re one of an integral asset for the business.
